Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I schedule a garden consultation?

Scheduling a garden consultation is easy! You can call our design team direct at +1 (888) 200-9898 or email design@botanicrhythm.com. We also accept requests via the contact form and will reply promptly to confirm your appointment.

2. What areas do you serve?

Our primary service area covers New York City and surrounding counties. We also take on projects in select upstate and neighboring states by special arrangement. Please inquire about availability.

3. Can I visit your garden demonstration site without an appointment?

For security and quality reasons, visits are by appointment only. Please contact us ahead of time so we can prepare your visit for a pleasant and safe experience.

4. What COVID-19 measures are in place?

We follow all local health guidelines to ensure the safety of our staff and clients. Onsite visits require masks and adherence to social distancing. We also offer virtual consultations for convenience.

5. How can I provide feedback?

We value your input! Submit feedback via our contact form, email us at support@botanicrhythm.com, or call customer support.

6. When can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond to inquiries within 24 to 48 hours during business days. Urgent requests can be flagged by phone.
